n8n & AI agents on connected data

Outcome: agents that can act safely

Problem: teams want AI to “do things,” but without mapped fields and stable integrations, agents hallucinate actions.

Approach: identify and map source-of-truth fields, expose them through n8n (or equivalent) nodes and HTTP steps, then layer AI agents with tool calls, approvals, and logging.
